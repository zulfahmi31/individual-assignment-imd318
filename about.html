About Me

Hello! I'm Muhammad Zulfahmi bin Zulkifli, a 20-year-old student currently in my final year of studying Information Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Sungai Petani branch. I was born in Sungai Petani, Kedah, and I am the third of four siblings in my family. With a keen interest in information systems, data management, and technology, I have always been fascinated by the way data can be used to solve real-world problems and drive business solutions. Throughout my studies, I have gained a strong foundation in these areas, and I am eager to apply my knowledge in practical settings after graduation. This website serves as a personal space where I share my academic journey, projects, and aspirations. I am excited to connect with like-minded individuals, collaborate on meaningful projects, and continue learning as I embark on my professional career. Feel free to reach out if you’re interested in discussing ideas, collaborations, or have any questions related to my field of study.

This is me, excited and ready to tackle new challenges!
