My Education

My educational journey began at Tadika Al-Islah As-Salam, where I developed a foundation for learning and curiosity. I then continued my primary education at SK Seri Gedong from 2011 to 2016, where I not only acquired essential academic skills but also discovered my early interest in technology and problem-solving. In 2017, I transitioned to SMK Bakar Arang, where I spent the next five years completing my SPM (Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia). During my time in secondary school, I became more focused on subjects related to information science and technology, which sparked my interest in the field of information management and computing. After completing my SPM, I was offered a place at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kedah, where I embarked on my Diploma in Information Management. This was an exciting new chapter, as it provided me with a deeper understanding of how information systems work and their critical role in the digital world. Currently, I am in my final year of pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Information Management at UiTM Kedah. Throughout my academic journey, I have honed my skills in data management and coding which have laid the foundation for my future career. As I near graduation, I am eager to apply my knowledge in real-world settings, especially in areas that combine my passion for technology, problem-solving, and business.
