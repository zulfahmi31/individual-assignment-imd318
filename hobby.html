My Hobbies

I love going on fishing trips with my father to Yan whenever I have some leisure time. My passion for fishing began at the age of seven, when I first accompanied my father on a fishing outing to Teluk Bayu. What fascinated me the most about fishing was the thrill of feeling the strong pull of the fishing rod in my hands when a fish strikes. That intense moment, when the line tugs sharply, has always been exhilarating, and it sparked a deep interest in the sport. Since then, fishing has become not just a hobby, but a cherished bonding experience with my father, and a way for me to unwind and connect with nature.
